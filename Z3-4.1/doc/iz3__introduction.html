An Efficient Theorem Prover

Introduction

iZ3 is an interpolating version of Z3 , the high-performance theorem prover being developed at Microsoft Research. iZ3 supports linear real and integer arithmetic, arrays, uninterpreted functions, and quantifiers. iZ3 can read interpolation problems in SMT-LIB format.
